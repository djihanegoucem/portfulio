About Me

As Djihane Goucem, a multimedia journalist, I’m dedicated to telling stories that inform, inspire, and amplify under-reported voices. Trained in multimedia and investigative storytelling, I approach every topic with rigor and strong journalistic integrity.

My reporting work has taken me across Algeria, Oman, Egypt, and Qatar, covering diverse issues and stories that matter beyond borders. Through video, audio, and written journalism, I aim to connect audiences to meaningful narratives while upholding ethical reporting.

Recognized as top of my academic cohort and serving as an Arab Youth Ambassador and UN Youth Delegate, I believe media is a powerful tool for positive change. For me, journalism is both a responsibility and a catalyst for shaping the conversations of tomorrow. I’m driven by impact—creating compelling stories and fostering dialogue through storytelling that matters.
